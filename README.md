## Topic: Under development kids prediction 🧒🏿🧒🏼🧒🏾🧒🏻🧒🏽

Our goals is to develop a ML model to classify children at risk of underdevelopment by analyzing data from UNICEF’s Multiple Indicator Cluster Surveys (MICS).
The goal is to identify vulnerable groups, enabling policymakers and organizations to allocate resources more effectively and implement targeted interventions.

By accurately classifying children at risk of underdevelopment, this project facilitates the strategic allocation of resources to areas and groups most in need.
Effective resource distribution is vital for implementing interventions that can significantly improve developmental outcomes, promoting equity and enhancing the well-being of vulnerable children.
